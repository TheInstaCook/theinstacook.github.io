Punjabi Chole recipe (Chickpea Curry) - The Insta Cook | पंजाबी छोले - द इंस्टा कुक

📅 2020-04-21 ⏱ 3:41 Misc Recipes

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Main ingredient (paneer/chicken/veg)
  • Fresh cream optional

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan and add panch phoron (five spice mix).
  2. Add the main vegetable/pumpkin and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame until tender.
  5. Add sugar (if using) and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
