📋 Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Main ingredient (paneer/chicken/veg)
  • Fresh cream optional
