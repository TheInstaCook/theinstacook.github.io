Bengali Kumror Chokka (Pumpkin Curry) Recipe — Curry by The Insta Cook

📅 2020-04-18 ⏱ 2:42 Bengali Recipes

📋 TL;DR

  • • Learn to make Bengali Kumror Chokka (Pumpkin Curry) — a curry ready in 2 minutes.
  • • Key ingredients: chilli, cook, cumin, curry, garam masala.
  • • Perfect for weeknight dinner — watch the full video for step-by-step guidance.

📋 Ingredients

  • 500g pumpkin (kumror), peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp panch phoron
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan and add panch phoron (five spice mix).
  2. Add the main vegetable/pumpkin and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  4. Cover and cook on low flame until tender.
  5. Add sugar (if using) and simmer for another 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
