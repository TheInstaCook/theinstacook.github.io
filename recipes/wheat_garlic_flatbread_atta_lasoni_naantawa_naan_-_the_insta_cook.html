👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, salt, and water.
  2. Knead into a soft dough and rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough into desired shape.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa or griddle until golden on both sides.
  5. Add toppings if making pizza-style.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.
