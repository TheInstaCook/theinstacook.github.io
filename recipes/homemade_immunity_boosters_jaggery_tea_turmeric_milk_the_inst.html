Homemade Immunity Boosters | Jaggery Tea | Turmeric Milk | The Insta Cook

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 inch fresh turmeric (grated)
  • 1 inch ginger (grated)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 black peppercorns
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 tsp jaggery/honey
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • For turmeric milk: 1 cup milk, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/4 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp honey

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For turmeric milk: Heat milk with turmeric, black pepper, and honey.
  2. Simmer for 3-4 minutes, strain, and drink warm.
  3. For jaggery tea: Boil water with ginger, turmeric, pepper, cloves.
  4. Add jaggery and lemon juice, simmer 2 minutes.
  5. For kadha: boil all spices in water for 10 minutes.
  6. Strain and consume warm for best immunity benefits.
