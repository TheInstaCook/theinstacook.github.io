Chocolate Truffle Recipe — Recipe by The Insta Cook

📋 Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70%+)
  • 100g condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews) for garnish
  • Oreo biscuits (crushed, optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Melt dark chocolate and butter together (double boiler or microwave).
  2. Mix in condensed milk and vanilla extract until smooth.
  3. Chill the mixture for 2-3 hours until firm.
  4. Scoop portions and roll into balls.
  5. Coat with cocoa powder, crushed nuts, or crushed Oreos.
  6. Refrigerate for 30 minutes and serve chilled.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
