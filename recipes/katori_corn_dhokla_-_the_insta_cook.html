Katori Corn Dhokla - The Insta Cook | कटोरी कॉर्न ढोकला - द इंस्टा कुक 🔥

📅 2020-05-15 ⏱ 4:00 Street Food at Home
A healthy Dhokla made up of sweetcorn and Suji / Semolina. Hope you will try this Katori Corn Dhokla at home. एक हेल्दी ढोकला, स्वीट कॉर्न और सूजी / सेमॅलीनॅ से बना। आशा है कि आप घर पर कोशिश करेंगे। Lots of Love, The Insta Cook Related Search Queries: #Recipe #GujaratiFood #EasyRecipe #Cooking #QuickRecipe #Kathiyawadi #Homemade #GujaratiRecipe

📋 Ingredients

  • Sweetcorn - 2 cups
  • Green Chilli - 4 nos
  • Curd - 1 cup
  • Fruit Salt (Eno) - 1 tsp

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Add Water
  2. Cover and rest for 10 minutes
  3. Sprinkle Red Chilli powder
  4. Can use Idli maker
