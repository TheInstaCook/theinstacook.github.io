Eggless Blooming Onion recipe in Indian style Recipe — Recipe by The Insta Cook

📅 2020-06-29 ⏱ 3:19 Street Food at Home

📋 TL;DR

  • • Learn to make Eggless Blooming Onion recipe in Indian style — a recipe ready in 3 minutes.
  • • Key ingredients: cook, fry, mix, onion, pakoda.
  • • Perfect for any meal — watch the full video for step-by-step guidance.

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil/ghee and add whole spices.
  3. Sauté onions until golden.
  4. Add main ingredients (veggies/chicken) and cook.
  5. Layer with rice and cook on low flame (dum).
  6. Garnish and serve hot.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
