Bengali Paneer Sabzi | Easy Paneer Curry in 20 Min 🔥

📅 2022-09-10 ⏱ 6:43 Bengali Recipes
In this video, learn how to make authentic Bengali Paneer Ki Sabzi (Paneer Curry) — a rich, flavourful Bengali-style paneer dish that pairs perfectly with rice or roti. 📌 INGREDIENTS: - Paneer — 250g, cubed - Potato (aloo) — 2 medium, cubed #PaneerLovers #BengaliCuisine #RiceRecipe #EastIndianFood #IndianBreakfast #PaneerRecipe #Vegetarian #BengaliFood #OnePotMeal #Flatbread #ComfortFood #StuffedParatha #IndianRice #ParathaRecipe #CottageCheese

📋 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream (optional)
