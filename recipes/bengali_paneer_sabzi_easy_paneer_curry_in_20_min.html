Bengali Paneer Sabzi | Easy Paneer Curry in 20 Min 🔥

📅 2022-09-10 ⏱ 6:43 Bengali Recipes
In this video, learn how to make authentic Bengali Paneer Ki Sabzi (Paneer Curry) — a rich, flavourful Bengali-style paneer dish that pairs perfectly with rice or roti. 📌 INGREDIENTS: - Paneer — 250g, cubed - Potato (aloo) — 2 medium, cubed #PaneerLovers #BengaliCuisine #RiceRecipe #EastIndianFood #IndianBreakfast #PaneerRecipe #Vegetarian #BengaliFood #OnePotMeal #Flatbread #ComfortFood #StuffedParatha #IndianRice #ParathaRecipe #CottageCheese

📋 Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer, cubed
  • 2 medium Potatoes, cubed
  • 2 tbsp Mustard oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 Tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Green Chillies, slit
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat mustard oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  5. Add turmeric, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chilli powder, and salt.
  6. Add cubed potatoes and green chillies. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add 1 cup water, cover and cook until potatoes are half done.
  8. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.
