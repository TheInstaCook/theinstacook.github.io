Upgrade the Paratha into a Pizza | Paratha Pizza recipe | बच्चों के लिए गेहूं पराठा पिज्जा

Upgrade the Paratha into a Pizza for your kids at home. This recipe is a simple idea to make our day to day paratha into something attractive for our kids. घर पर अपने बच्चों के लिए पराठे को पिज्जा में अपग्रेड करें। Lots of Love, The Insta Cook Related Search Queries: #PaneerLovers #BengaliCuisine #Recipe #EastIndianFood #TraditionalRecipe #IndianBreakfast #PaneerRecipe #Vegetarian #BengaliFood #Flatbread #StuffedParatha #Cooking #ParathaRecipe #CottageCheese

📋 Ingredients

  • Red Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • Yellow Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • Cottage Cheese (Paneer) (diced)
  • Boiled Sweetcorn
  • Wheat flour (Atta) dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Chili Flakes
  • Oregano

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients: chop red bell pepper and yellow bell pepper, dice cottage cheese (paneer), and ensure sweetcorn is boiled. Have wheat flour (atta) dough, pizza sauce, grated cheese, chili flakes, and oregano ready.
  2. Place a wheat flour (atta) dough paratha on a hot griddle (tava). Cook it until partially done, flipping as necessary.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the cooked paratha.
  4. Add the prepared toppings: red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, cottage cheese (paneer), and boiled sweetcorn.
  5. Generously add grated cheese over the toppings.
  6. Cover the paratha with a lid and cook on low-medium heat until the cheese melts completely.
  7. Remove the lid, then sprinkle chili flakes and oregano over the melted cheese and toppings.
