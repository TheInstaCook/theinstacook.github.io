📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Few saffron strands
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp curd/yogurt
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix flour, besan, turmeric, and curd to form a smooth batter.
  2. Let the batter ferment for 4-6 hours.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom and saffron.
  4. Fill the batter in a piping bag and pip spirals into hot oil.
  5. Fry until golden and crispy, then soak in sugar syrup.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
