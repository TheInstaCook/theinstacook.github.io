World's New Recipe | Father's Day Special | Father's Day Gift | Chocolate Barfi using ParleG biscuit

📅 2020-06-12 ⏱ 2:54 Indian Sweets & Desserts

📋 Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70%+)
  • 100g condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, cashews) for garnish
  • Oreo biscuits (crushed, optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients as shown in the video.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan on medium flame.
  3. Cook the main mixture while stirring continuously.
  4. Add sugar and flavorings as per preference.
  5. Shape or set the mixture as shown.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve.
