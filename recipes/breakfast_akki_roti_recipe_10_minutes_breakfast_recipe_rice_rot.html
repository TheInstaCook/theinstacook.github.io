Breakfast Akki Roti Recipe | 10 minutes Breakfast Recipe | Rice Rotti | The Insta Cook

📅 2020-07-23 ⏱ 3:01 Breakfast Recipes

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, curd, baking soda, sugar, salt, oil, and water.
  2. Knead into a soft dough and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. For stuffing: mix grated paneer with onions, chilies, spices, and coriander.
  4. Roll out dough, place stuffing in center, seal edges, and roll again gently.
  5. Cook on hot tawa with ghee/butter until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with chutney, curd, or butter.
