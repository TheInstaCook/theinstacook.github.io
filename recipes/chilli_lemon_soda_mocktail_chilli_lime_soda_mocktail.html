Chilli Lemon Soda Mocktail | Chilli Lime Soda Mocktail | मसालेदार नींबू मॉकटेल - द इंस्टा कुक

📅 2020-04-24 ⏱ 2:03 Drinks & Mocktails

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp black salt
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp sugar or jaggery
  • 1 cup soda water / sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Muddle lime, lemon, green chilies, mint, and sugar in a glass.
  2. Add black salt, roasted cumin powder, and mix well.
  3. Fill glass with ice cubes.
  4. Top up with chilled soda water / sparkling water.
  5. Stir gently and garnish with mint leaves.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing drink!
