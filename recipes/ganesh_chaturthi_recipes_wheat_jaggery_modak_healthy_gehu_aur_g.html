Ganesh Chaturthi recipes Recipe — Recipe by The Insta Cook

📅 2020-08-18 ⏱ 2:59 Indian Sweets & Desserts

📋 TL;DR

  • • Learn to make Ganesh Chaturthi recipes — a recipe ready in 2 minutes.
  • • Key ingredients: cardamom, flour, fry, ghee, jaggery.
  • • Perfect for any meal — watch the full video for step-by-step guidance.

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup jaggery
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients as shown in the video.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan on medium flame.
  3. Cook the main mixture while stirring continuously.
  4. Add sugar and flavorings as per preference.
  5. Shape or set the mixture as shown.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
