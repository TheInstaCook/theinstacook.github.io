Gujarat Famous Aloo Puri Recipe — Recipe by The Insta Cook

📅 2020-04-28 ⏱ 4:25 Breakfast Recipes

📋 TL;DR

  • • Learn to make Gujarat Famous Aloo Puri — a recipe ready in 4 minutes.
  • • Key ingredients: black salt, cook, fry, garlic, knead.
  • • Perfect for any meal — watch the full video for step-by-step guidance.

📋 Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup boiled potato (mashed)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Water for dough

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the base (katori/bowl) as shown in the video.
  2. Boil or cook the main ingredients (potatoes, chickpeas).
  3. Arrange in serving dish with chutneys.
  4. Top with curd, sev, and spices.
  5. Garnish and serve immediately.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
