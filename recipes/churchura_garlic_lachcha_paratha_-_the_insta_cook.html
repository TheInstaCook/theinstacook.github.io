Churchura Garlic Lachcha Paratha Recipe — Paratha by The Insta Cook

📅 2020-04-23 ⏱ 4:05 Breakfast Recipes

📋 TL;DR

  • • Learn to make Churchura Garlic Lachcha Paratha — a paratha ready in 4 minutes.
  • • Key ingredients: chop, cook, garlic, ghee, knead.
  • • Perfect for hearty breakfast — watch the full video for step-by-step guidance.

📋 Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Stuffing of choice (aloo, paneer, etc.)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, curd, baking soda, sugar, salt, oil, and water.
  2. Knead into a soft dough and rest for 30 minutes.
  3. For stuffing: mix grated paneer with onions, chilies, spices, and coriander.
  4. Roll out dough, place stuffing in center, seal edges, and roll again gently.
  5. Cook on hot tawa with ghee/butter until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with chutney, curd, or butter.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
