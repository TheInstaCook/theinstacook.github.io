📋 Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 500 gm
  • Garlic (chopped) - 1 tbsp
  • Onion (chopped) - 2 medium
  • Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Tomato sauce - 2 tbsp
  • Soya granules
  • Oregano - 1 tsp

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil Water
  2. Normally it takes 15 minutes to cook
  3. Check if done
  4. Add little Salt
  5. Add the soaked Soya granules
  6. Add the Spaghetti
  7. Salt to taste
