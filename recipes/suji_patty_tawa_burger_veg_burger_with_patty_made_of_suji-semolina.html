👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat full-fat milk to near boiling (90-95°C).
  2. Add lemon juice/vinegar diluted in hot water, stir gently.
  3. Milk will curdle - separate whey from curds using a muslin cloth.
  4. Rinse curds with cold water to remove sourness.
  5. Knead the curds in hot water until stretchy and smooth.
  6. Shape into balls, cool in ice water, then store in brine.

💡 Tips & Variations

  • 💡 Pro Tip: Use chilled soda water for maximum fizz in mocktails.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Rest dough for 30 minutes for softer kulchas/parathas.
  • 💡 Pro Tip: Adjust spice level by adding/removing chilli seeds.
  • 💡 Variation: Add grated cheese to stuffing for extra richness.
  • 💡 Storage: Best consumed fresh; refrigerate leftovers for 1-2 days.
