Crispy Fried Corn Chaat recipe | Crispy Chaat recipe | Healthy Tasty recipe | The Insta Cook

📅 2020-08-09 ⏱ 2:33 Street Food at Home

📋 Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas
  • 1 cup curd/yogurt
  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup green chutney
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Sev for garnish
  • Chopped onion, tomato, coriander
  • Salt and red chili powder to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the base (katori/bowl) as shown in the video.
  2. Boil or cook the main ingredients (potatoes, chickpeas).
  3. Arrange in serving dish with chutneys.
  4. Top with curd, sev, and spices.
  5. Garnish and serve immediately.
