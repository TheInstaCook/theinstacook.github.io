📋 Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Mix of veggies (onion, capsicum)
  • Chaat masala to taste
  • Coriander chutney
