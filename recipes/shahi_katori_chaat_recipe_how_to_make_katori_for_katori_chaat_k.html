Shahi Katori Chaat Recipe | How to Make Katori for Katori chaat | Katori chaat recipe in hindi

📋 Ingredients

  • Wheat flour (Atta)
  • Salt
  • hot Oil
  • Water
  • boiled Sweet corn
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Capsicum
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Bell Pepper
  • Coriander chutney
  • Tamarind paste
  • Curd
  • Chaat masala
  • Black salt
  • Coriander leaves
  • Green Chillies

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Add Wheat flour (Atta), Salt and hot Oil.
  2. Add Water
  3. Add boiled Sweet corn, Onion, Tomato, Cucumber, Capsicum, Red & Yellow Bell Pepper, Coriander chutney, Tamarind paste, Curd, Chaat masala, Black salt, Coriander leaves, Green Chillies.
