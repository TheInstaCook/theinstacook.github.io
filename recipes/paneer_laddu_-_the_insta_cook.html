📋 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients as shown in the video.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan on medium flame.
  3. Cook the main mixture while stirring continuously.
  4. Add sugar and flavorings as per preference.
  5. Shape or set the mixture as shown.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve.
