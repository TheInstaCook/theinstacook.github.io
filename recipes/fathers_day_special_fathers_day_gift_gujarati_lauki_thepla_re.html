Father's Day Special | Father's Day Gift | Gujarati Lauki Thepla recipe | Dudhi na Thepla

📅 2020-06-18 ⏱ 3:39 Breakfast Recipes
Make tasty Thepla at home with Lauki / Dudhi. Hope you all will like this Gujarati Lauki Thepla recipe. लौकी / दूधी के साथ घर पर स्वादिष्ट थेपला बनाएं। आशा है आप सभी को यह गुजराती लौकी थेपला रेसिपी पसंद आएगी। Lots of Love, The Insta Cook Related Search Queries: #Recipe #HomemadeBread #BreadLover #BreadRecipe #GujaratiFood #EasyRecipe #Cooking #QuickRecipe #Baking #Kathiyawadi #Homemade #GujaratiRecipe

📋 Ingredients

  • Curd/Dahi 1/2 cup
  • Turmeric
  • Mustard seeds 1 tsp
  • Curry leaves 8 to 10 nos
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Grind to a paste
  2. Mix well
  3. Put the Thepla over a hot Tawa Flip and coat with Groundnut oil Flip and apply oil on other side
