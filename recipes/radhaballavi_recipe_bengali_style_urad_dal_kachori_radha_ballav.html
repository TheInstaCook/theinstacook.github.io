Radhaballavi Recipe | Bengali Style Urad Dal Kachori | Radha Ballavi Recipe - The Insta Cook

📅 2020-04-30 ⏱ 3:53 Bengali Recipes
Radhaballavi / Radhaballabhi or Bengali style Stuffed Urad dal puri is undoubtedly one of the favourite recipe of a typical Bengali Household. Try this Bengali style Urad Dal Kachori recipe at home. राधाबल्लभी या भरवां उरद दाल की पुरी निस्संदेह एक विशिष्ट बंगाली घराने के पसंदीदा भोजन में से एक है। इसे घर पर ट्राई करें। Lots of Love, The Insta Cook Related Search Queries: #QuickBites #BengaliCuisine #Recipe #DalRecipe #EastIndianFood #TraditionalRecipe #Lentils #BengaliFood #SnackRecipe #ComfortFood #Cooking #TeaTimeSnacks #EveningSnacks #HealthyEating

📋 Ingredients

  • Urad Dal / Biulir Dal (Soaked for 4-5 hours)
  • Green Chillies: 6 nos
  • Ginger: 1 inch (3 nos)
  • Fennel seeds / Mouri: 2 tsp (grind)
  • Refined flour / Maida: 2 cups
  • Fennel-seeds (ground): 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar: 1/2 tsp
  • Asafoetida / Hing: 1/2 tsp
  • Fennel seeds-powder: 2 tsp

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Soak Urad Dal / Biulir Dal for 4-5 hours.
  2. Grind to a paste with ginger, green chillies, and 2 tsp fennel seeds.
  3. Take Refined flour / Maida - 2 cups.
  4. Add Fennel-seeds (ground) - 1 tsp to the flour.
  5. Add Salt & Sugar - 1/2 tsp each to the flour.
  6. Add the paste from step 2 to the flour.
  7. Add Water and knead.
  8. Cover and rest for 10 mins.
  9. Add Asafoetida / Hing - 1/2 tsp to a pan.
  10. Add Ginger-Chilli paste to the pan.
  11. Add Fennel seeds-powder - 2 tsp to the pan.
  12. Add the Dal-paste to the pan and cook.
