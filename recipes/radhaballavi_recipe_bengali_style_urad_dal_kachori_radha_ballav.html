Radhaballavi / Radhaballabhi or Bengali style Stuffed Urad dal puri is undoubtedly one of the favourite recipe of a typical Bengali Household. 📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup toor dal / moong dal
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare the base (katori/bowl) as shown in the video.
  2. Boil or cook the main ingredients (potatoes, chickpeas).
  3. Arrange in serving dish with chutneys.
  4. Top with curd, sev, and spices.
  5. Garnish and serve immediately.
