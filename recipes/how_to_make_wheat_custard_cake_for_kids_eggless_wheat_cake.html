How to make Wheat Custard Cake for Kids | Eggless Wheat Cake | बच्चों के लिए व्हीट कस्टर्ड केक बनाये

📅 2020-05-28 ⏱ 4:08 Indian Sweets & Desserts

📋 Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup oil or butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Prepare all ingredients as shown in the video.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan on medium flame.
  3. Cook the main mixture while stirring continuously.
  4. Add sugar and flavorings as per preference.
  5. Shape or set the mixture as shown.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve.
