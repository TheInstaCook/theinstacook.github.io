Breakfast Wheat Aloo Masala Poori for kids | बच्चों के लिए आलू मसाला पूरी | Gehu ka Masala Puri

📅 2020-06-02 ⏱ 2:00 Breakfast Recipes
How to make Alu Masala Puri for breakfast for your kids. Hope you will like the recipe. अपने बच्चों के लिए घर पर आलू मसाला पूरी कैसे बनाएं। आशा है आपको रेसिपी पसंद आएगी। Lots of Love, The Insta Cook Related Search Queries: #DhabaStyle #Recipe #EasyRecipe #Cooking #PunjabiFood #QuickRecipe #NorthIndian #Homemade

📋 Ingredients

  • Boiled Potatoes - 7 to 8 medium
  • Wheat Flour / Atta - 2 cups
  • Suji / Semolina - 1/2 cup
  • Kasuri Methi - 2 tsp
  • Red Chilli powder - 1 tsp
  • Ajwain / Carom seeds- 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix well
  2. Add Water
  3. Add little Oil and knead
  4. Roll to a round shape
