📋 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream (optional)
